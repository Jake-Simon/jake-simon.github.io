Star Giant Productions Contributions

Star Giant Productions was a YouTube channel owned by Rebeccah Starlight. Her videos mostly involve ranking and reviewing Western Animation based on a specific topic. I work as one of several editors for her channel from November 2021 to March 2024 under the name SageFroppy.

Highest Grossing Animated Films Ranked

This is Rebeccah's 10th anniversary video for the Star Giant Productions channel where she ranks the top 50 highest grossing animated films from How to Train Your Dragon 3 with about $525 million to The Lion King (2019) with at least $1.66 billion, in terms of preference. It is also the biggest project I made a contribution to in terms of general video length I edited for, being almost 57 minutes. Note that the films she ranks are based on her findings in January of 2023, even though the video was released in December of the same year. As such, she does not explain her rankings with The Grinch (2018), The Super Mario Bros. Movie (2023), or Spider-Man: Across the Spiderverse. My sections deal mostly with what Disney currently owns, but I have edited segments talking about the DreamWorks films Shrek the Third and Madagascar. One of my noticeable edits is incorporating the King Crimson (JoJo's Bizarre Adventure) effects in The Lion King segment I edited at 0:42:26.

FilmAcademy360 Contributions

FilmAcademy360 is a film studio that is part of the Academy360 Upper School (run by Spectrum360) in Livingston, New Jersey, and is run by David DiIanni. Many of the videos produced by the studio are created by students in the program and published on the Media Crunch YouTube Channel.  I have contributed to FilmAcademy360 since 2015 and am currently working as an intern since 2020.  Throughout my time in FilmAcademy360, I have created my own show on the Youtube Channel called "Jake's Animated Review Show", where I discuss anime. During my Animated Review Show, I also have a contribution to help with camerawork and editing.

Golf Outing 2023

This practice project is a montage video showcasing the different aspects of the 2023 Fore the Kids Golf Tournament. While provided with the same B-Roll, the montage was made separately from what FilmAcademy360 used to promote the country club.
