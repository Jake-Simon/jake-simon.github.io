FilmAcademy360 Contributions

FilmAcademy360 is a film studio that is part of the Academy360 Upper School in Livingston, New Jersey, and is run by David DiIanni. Many of the videos produced by the studio are created by students in the program and published on the Media Crunch YouTube Channel.  I have contributed to FilmAcademy360 since 2015 and am currently working as an intern since 2020.  Throughout my time in FilmAcademy360, I have created my own show on the Youtube Channel called "Jake's Animated Review Show", where I discuss anime. During my Animated Review Show, I also have a contribution to help with camerawork and editing.

Jake's Animated Review Show: JoJo's Bizarre Adventure

This is my review of the JoJo's Bizarre Adventure anime. The video was made as a result of the original canon being completed for the anime. This is one of the most thoroughly planned episodes for my animated review show, as it incorporates me doing different poses and finding different images to show the reference that I pose with. My favorite portion is renacting the "Approaching Me" scene from Season 2, with some slight tweaks.

Jake's Animated Review Show: One Piece

This is my review of the One Piece anime. The video was made as a result of the anime reaching over 1000 episodes. One interesting thing I have added to the series was the intro being similar to the SpongeBob theme song.
